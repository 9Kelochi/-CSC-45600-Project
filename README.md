# -CSC-45600-Project

Project Name: NoteStudy

Project Pitch: NoteStudy is a course-based platform that helps college students to pass their classes by finding new/old notes left by the seniors or current classmates, and helping them find study partners, making studying more collaborative and efficient.

Project Description

NoteStudy is designed to help students better manage and share academic notes within specific courses. Instead of just relying on private group chats or scattered files, students can use NoteStudy to find organized notes uploaded by classmates or previous course students who are taking the same course with the same teacher. This ensures that shared content stays relevant, structured, and easy to access. The platform promotes collaboration and academic support by allowing students to contribute notes and review shared materials.

Primary Users:

-College and university students

-Students enrolled in shared courses

-Students who want collaborative study resources

Features:

-User registration and authentication

-Course-based organization of notes

-Upload and download lecture notes

-View and browse notes shared by classmates or sen

-Rating or feedback system for notes

-Private chat for collaborative study

Benefits

-Helps students catch up on missed lectures

-Improves study efficiency and understanding

-Encourages academic collaboration

-Keeps notes organized and easy to access
